So what are the rules?

Before the start of the season, pick your top 3 teams and top 3 overall drivers championship finishing positions.

Each week you will then:

  1. Pick your podium prediction (before qualifying/ideally no later than before FP3)
  2. Pick a "Wildcard" podium, this is your second chance at predicting a podium, but you cannot re-use any drivers from step 1. (again no later than FP3).

The important bit, how are points awarded? It's simple see the table below:

Main prediction Wildcard prediction
1st place points: 3 Points 1.5 Points
2nd place points: 2 Points 1 Points
3rd place points: 1 Points 0.5 Points
Match all 3?!! 1 additional Point for main 1 for wildcard fullhouse (it ain't likely gonna happen)
Best you can get... 7 points (all three main baby!) 4 points (you wild!!)

So what happens at the end of the season?

The winner is crowned as the ultimate champion and gets a free ride go-karting (and 1 free beer from each of the other contestants??!)
